Important information about your direct debit payments

Dear <%= recipient.firstName %>,

You currently pay for your insurance policy above by direct debit from a Ulster Bank or KBC Bank account. As you may know, <%= recipient.text3 %> have announced their intention to exit the Irish market in the coming months.

What does this mean for me?
You will need to update your bank account details so you can continue to pay your insurance premium by direct debit. You can do this on MyAllianz by following these simple steps:

  • Once logged into your MyAllianz account, select the “Menu” button beside the policy which you would like to update payment details for.
  • Navigate to the “Request Changes” option.
  • Select the “Update My Bank Details” option and input your BIC & IBAN as requested on the screen.

If you don’t have a MyAllianz Account, simply click on this link and follow the instructions. Once you have done this, we will update your new bank account information and send you confirmation. If you would like to update your details over the phone you can call our Instalment Billing Team on 01 613 3909 between 9am and 5pm Monday to Friday.

Just to note, if a direct debit is due within 7–10 days of making this change it may not be possible to update your bank account in time for the next direct debit collection from your bank account.
